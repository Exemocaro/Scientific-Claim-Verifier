Understanding Similarity Scores: All visualizations use semantic similarity (0.0-1.0) calculated as 1/(1+distance) where distance is the L2 distance between embedding vectors. Higher scores mean propositions are semantically closer to your query. Color coding: Red (0.0-0.5) = low similarity, Yellow (~0.5) = moderate, Green (0.5-1.0) = high similarity. This measures meaning alignment, not factual correctness.
